B.Sc Nursing

Course Description

The duration of the course for B.Sc Nursing shall be 4 years including internship. The course commences from 1st august of the academic year. University examinations are conducted at the end of the year (non semester pattern). Medium of instruction is English for all the subjects of the study and for examination. Jennys college of nursing provides rich clinical experience and students will be posted in different tie up hospitals.

Eligibility for Admission for B.Sc nursing (4 years)

1. Candidate should have passed the qualifying examination (Hsc/Pre degree) after a period of 12 years of study under a recognized board/university with science(Physics, Chemistry and Biology) and English with minimum of 45% aggregate marks in above mentioned 4 subjects (PCBE) and for Sc/St Candidates, the mark in PCBE subject is 40%
2. Minimum age of admission shall be 17 years on or before 31st December of the years admission
3. Only unmarried female candidates are eligible to apply
4. The candidate shall be medically fit.
5. Eligibility Certificate:
The Candidates who has passed any qualifying examination, other than the higher secondary course examination conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu, before seeking admission, shall obtain an eligibility Certificate from the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University by remitting the prescribed fees along with the application form which shall be downloaded from the University website www.tnmgrmu.ac.in).
