Jennys College of Nursing

Recognised by Indian Nursing Council, Tamilnadu Nurses & Midwives Council & Affiliated to the Tamilnadu Dr.M.G.R Medical University, Chennai.

The Smt. Rani Adaikalaraj Education and Charitable Trust was started in the year 1996 under the vision of our founder Thiru. L. Adaikalaraj Ex. Member of Parliament from Trichy Constituency for four consecutive terms. It was his vision to provide quality education to the masses so that the youth would get the opportunity to equip themselves with knowledge and skills by which they become contributing citizens for their families and also the society, thereby building the nation.

  • Jenneys College of Nursing was started in the year 2010 preparing nurses with the highest level of education and practice.
  • With the aim to provide compassionate, caring, skilled and well trained nurses with the focus to serve the weak and sick with utmost care by providing quality nursing education.
  • And preparing nurses with the highest level of education and practice.

Why Choose Jennys Nursing College?

Education shapes minds and builds life. As nursing education is a divine call, re-aligning one to the higher order vocation becomes a mandate. Jenneys College of Nursing with its comprehensive vision facilitate all round development of the student thereby it blends the raw minds of students into tender yet dexterous Nurses who become a part of a future benevolent community.
