Why Jennys College of Nursing?

FACULTY

Full Fledged qualified and experienced staff working consistently for more than 10 years.

PHYSICAL INFRASTRUCTURE

Jennys College of Nursing is constructed on a sprawling campus and has an excellent physical infrastructure as per the Indian Nursing Council norms. The well equipped infrastructure encompasses Academic Blocks, Administrative Blocks, Faculty Rooms, Library and Laboratories. All the Classrooms are spacious, ventilated and equipped with LCD Projector.

CLINICALS

To provide vast and rich experience for the students we have tie up facilities with Govt. Head quarters Hospital Trichy, Southern Railway Hospital, Trichy, Athma Educational and Research Foundation.
Affiliated with primary Health Centre, Enamkulathur, and Periyamilaguparai the Urban Health Centre for the students to gain experience in various rural and Urban Government Health Programmes. We have adopted Nachikurichi village which has a Population of about 12,000 to render Rural Health services through our Jennys Community Health centre.

HOSTEL

Separate Girls Hostel is available with well ventilated, spacious rooms, safe for student accommodation. It is equipped with reading rooms, recreation hall, visitors hall, sick room, Hygienic Kitchen with R.O water plant and solar system Hot water facility.

LABORATORY

Laboratories are available for all the specialities, adequately equipped with mannequins, models, articles and equipments to provide hands on experience for the students in a controlled setting.

LIBRARY

JCON Library is a house of Knowledge stocking more than 4000 books and subscriptions about 15 National and International journals. It also has a digital Library with e - journals facility.

Jennys College of Nursing

The Smt. Rani Adaikalaraj Educational & Charitable Trust was established in 1996 to serve the youth of this country by providing quality education under the vision of our founder Thiru. L. Adaikalaraj Ex. Member of Parliament. Jennys College of Nursing was started in 2010 with the mission to contribute a hand in taking care of the weak and sick by providing quality Nursing Education.
