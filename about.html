About Us

The Smt. Rani Adaikalaraj Education and Charitable Trust was started in the year 1996 under the vision of our founder Thiru. L. Adaikalaraj Ex. Member of Parliament from Trichy Constituency for four consecutive terms.

It was his vision to provide quality education to the masses so that the youth would get the opportunity to equip themselves with knowledge and skills by which they become contributing citizens for their families and also the society, thereby building the nation.

Jenneys College of Nursing was started in the year 2010 with the aim to provide compassionate, caring, skilled and well trained nurses with the focus to serve the weak and sick with utmost care by providing quality nursing education and preparing nurses with the highest level of education and practice.

The College is affiliated to the TamilNadu Dr. MGR Medical University, Chennai and is recognised by the Indian Nursing Council (INC) New Delhi, and the Tamil Nadu Nurses and Midwives Council, Chennai. The eminent faculty members and staff are committed to develop nursing personnel through quality education and training.

Vision

To become a premier provider in Nursing Education of International repute and emerge as a centre that is recognized for excellence in Education, Service, Research and Leadership with commitment to The Trust’s core values.

Mission

To establish Jennys College of Nursing as an eminent centre for pursuing nursing education by placing student learning and practice as the top priority. To mould the students to serve with love and compassion to the ailing segments of the society.

Philosophy

The Smt. Rani Adaikalaraj educational and charitable trust inspired by the spirit of Christ’s belief that our service to the sick and needy must be based on the values of love and compassion.

We believe that Nursing is a profession that requires knowledge, skills and attitude based on values to provide holistic health care to individuals, family and community.

We believe that the basic course in nursing requires a good understanding of scientific principles.

We believe that high quality education transforms lives and students should be prepared to actively participate in a global community. We believe the graduates of the program will advocate to promote health, well-being and optimal quality of life through a diverse, multicultural society through practice of nursing in collaboration with other healthcare professionals.

We strive to inculcate the spirit of genuine love and true service in the hearts of the students. We believe research, education, and service are the foundation for professional nursing practice.

The Jennys College of Nursing believes these guiding principles form a belief system which is foundational to our Educational program.

Objectives

1. Applies knowledge from physical, biological and behavioral sciences, medicine including alternative systems in providing nursing care to individuals, families and community.
2. Provides nursing care using critical thinking skills based on steps of the nursing process and utilizes the latest technology and trends in providing health care.
3. Provides preventive, promotive and restorative health services in line with the National health policies and program.
4. Communicates effectively with individuals, groups and members of the health team and demonstrates skills in teaching to individuals and groups.
5. Practices within the framework of code of ethics and professional conduct and acceptable standards of practice within the legal boundaries.
6. Demonstrate leadership and managerial skills and conduct need based research study in clinical/community health setting to improve the quality of services.
